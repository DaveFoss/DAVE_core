These gas grid informations for the high pressure level are based on the data from the publication "Electricity, Heat, and Gas Sector Data for Modeling the German System". 
This Paper is available at https://www.diw.de/de/diw_01.c.574115.de/publikationen/data_documentation/2017_0092/electricity__heat_and_gas_sector_data_for_modelling_the_german_system.html  (last check 31.05.2020)

The Shape files are available at https://zenodo.org/record/1044463#.XsfjVMBCRhF (last check 31.05.2020)

The reference year for the data is 2015
